## What It Is

driveCREATE is a $10 digital bundle designed to take Oklahoma teen drivers from nervous passenger to confident driver. It covers everything the Oklahoma DPS permit process requires — study guides, defensive driving blueprints, parent co-pilot strategy, visual infographics, and a complete study workbook.

Built for the families doing Parent-Taught Driver Education (PTDE) through Service Oklahoma.

---

## What's Inside the Bundle

- **Oklahoma Driver Blueprint** — every requirement, fee, and GDL deadline in order
- **Defensive Driving Blueprint** — 3-second rule, blind spot elimination, hydroplaning recovery
- **Mindset Upgrade System** — box breathing, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, cortisol management
- **Parent Co-Pilot Strategy** — empty lot drills, commentary driving, the no-raised-voices rule
- **15+ Visual Infographics** — GDL timeline, signs decoder, right-of-way, danger zones (printable)
- **Complete Study Workbook** — condensed, practical, built for real life
